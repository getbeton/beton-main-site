## Why HealthTech Companies Need Revenue Intelligence

Healthcare technology adoption happens at the department level, then spreads across organizations. Identifying when a single-department customer is ready for organization-wide deployment is critical for expansion revenue.

## How Beton Works for HealthTech

Beton monitors clinical workflow adoption, cross-department usage patterns, and compliance feature activation to identify accounts that are ready for enterprise-level conversations.

## Common HealthTech Signals

- New departments onboarding within the same health system
- Clinical workflow completion rates exceeding benchmarks
- Compliance and audit trail features being activated
- Integration setup with EHR systems
- Admin users exploring enterprise configuration options
